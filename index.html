Our Story

Nurturing Knowledge,
Character & Faith
for Every Generation

Established in 2015 in Moodbidri, Al-Islah Residential College is dedicated to providing students with a balanced education rooted in Islamic values, academic learning, discipline, and personal growth in a safe and nurturing environment.

Our History
Since 2015, Al-Islah has been committed to guiding students through meaningful Islamic and academic education.
Our Mission
To nurture students with strong Islamic values, beneficial knowledge, good character, and responsibility towards society.
Our Vision
To build a generation of confident, disciplined, and knowledgeable students who positively contribute to the country.
Our Values
Faith, discipline, sincerity, compassion, and a commitment to lifelong learning and service.
