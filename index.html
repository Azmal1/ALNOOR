Our Story

Building Scholars
for This World
& the Next

Founded in 2015 by visionary scholars in Moodbidri Al-Islah has grown from a humble madrasa into Karnataka's leading center of Islamic learning — blending timeless tradition with the demands of the modern world.

Our History
Four decades of nurturing generations of scholars and leaders from across India.
Our Mission
Cultivating pious, knowledgeable individuals firmly rooted in Qur'an and Sunnah.
Our Vision
India's most respected modern Madrasa — tradition meets 21st-century excellence.
Our Values
Taqwa, excellence, community, and compassionate service to all of humanity.
